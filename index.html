How to ride an e-scooter or e-bike

Find a ride

Find a scooter on the street or locate one using the map in your Bikeshare app.

Unlock with app

Use the bikeshareapp to unlock the scooter or bike and start your ride.

Follow safety rules

Follow the traffic rules. Use bike lanes and wear a helmet to stay safe when riding.

Park with care

Park your e-scooter or e-bike away from public pathways - your community will appreciate it.
